# ☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard - Excel Data Visualization Project

## 📊 Project Overview

This project features a **dynamic Excel dashboard** designed to visualize and analyze coffee sales data across multiple dimensions such as time, country, product type, and customer activity. The dashboard provides actionable insights for business stakeholders by using slicers, interactive charts, and filters to explore trends and performance metrics.

---

## 📁 Files Included

* `coffeeOrdersData.xlsx` – The excel file containing the dataset, graphs and the dashboard.
* `Dataset.xlsx` – The main dataset used for the dashboard.
* `Excel Dashboard.png` – A snapshot of the final dashboard for quick viewing.

---

## 🔍 Key Features

* **Interactive Timeline Filter:** Allows users to select custom date ranges (from May 2019 to August 2022).
* **Sales Trend Visualization:** "Total Sales Over Time" line chart showing monthly trends by coffee variety (Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, Excelsa).
* **Country-wise Sales Analysis:** Bar chart highlighting top-performing countries, with the United States leading in total sales.
* **Customer Insights:** Lists the **Top 5 Customers** based on total spend.
* **Product Filters:** Slicers for Roast Type, Size, and Loyalty Card to deep-dive into specific customer/product segments.
* **Clean & Consistent UI:** A visually cohesive dark-themed layout designed for clarity and presentation.

---

## 📈 Insights Derived

* **The U.S. is the dominant market**, contributing over 80% of total sales.
* **Arabica and Excelsa varieties** show significant sales peaks, indicating customer preference or seasonal promotions.
* **Loyalty card holders** and **specific sizes** may impact sales trends (can be further explored using slicers).
